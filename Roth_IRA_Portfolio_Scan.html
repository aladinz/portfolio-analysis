Key Findings

Ranked diagnostic flags from critical to passing

Critical
Zero inflation / real-asset protection
100% equity, no TIPS, no commodities, no real assets. In a stagflationary regime — the one environment where both growth stocks and bonds fail together — this portfolio has no defensive anchor. Gold, commodity ETFs, or TIPS would serve as structural insurance.
Critical
Hidden semiconductor concentration ~35% effective
Surface allocation: SMH 12% + AVGO 3% = 15%. After FZROX look-through (tech ~28% of index × 41%), effective tech/semi exposure reaches ~35%. AVGO is simultaneously a direct holding AND a top-5 SMH constituent — a double-counted bet.
Warning
VRT — single-stock idiosyncratic risk at 6.5%
Vertiv is a high-conviction AI data center play but carries extreme single-stock risk. VRT fell –70% in 2022. At 6.5% of the Roth IRA, a –70% VRT move causes a –4.5% portfolio hit from this position alone. Position size is justified only if conviction is very high and time horizon is long (5+ years).
Warning
Cash at 2.5% — thin rebalancing buffer
In a sharp drawdown (–30%), 2.5% cash allows minimal tactical buying. Roth IRA contributions are capped annually, so dry powder is precious. Consider keeping 4–5% cash to deploy meaningfully during corrections.
Warning
SCHD / FZROX redundancy (0.88 correlation)
SCHD's dividend holdings (KO, PEP, JNJ, PFE, CVX) are all inside FZROX. SCHD contributes a useful value/income tilt and lower beta, but at 0.88 correlation it is not a true diversifier. Its primary value is the factor tilt, not independence.
Info
International underweight (10% VXUS)
Global market cap weight for ex-US equities is ~40%. At 10%, this portfolio is significantly US-centric. This is a deliberate tilt — not necessarily wrong — but means no structural benefit from dollar weakness or non-US growth cycles.
Pass
XLV — genuine structural diversifier
Healthcare exhibits low correlation with tech/semis and performs relatively well in rate-shock and recession regimes. XLV at 13% is one of the few true risk-off buffers in this portfolio. Size is appropriate.
Pass
FZROX — cost-efficient core
0% expense ratio. Total market coverage. Ideal as the portfolio's largest position. No action needed — this is the right anchor holding for a long-term Roth IRA.
