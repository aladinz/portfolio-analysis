Eliminated Position

Case for removal with capital reallocation

AVGO

Broadcom — Double-Counted Semiconductor Bet

AVGO is already a top-5 holding in SMH at approximately 8% weight. Owning AVGO outright at 3% alongside SMH at 12% means you effectively hold ~4% equivalent AVGO exposure from SMH alone — plus the 3% direct position. Total AVGO exposure: ~4% hidden + 3% explicit = ~7% in a single semiconductor name.

Eliminating the direct AVGO position does not reduce your AVGO exposure to zero — it normalises it back to the SMH index weight. The semi thesis is still fully expressed through SMH. Capital freed (3%) was split: 2% into IAU, 0.5% into cash, and 0.5% absorbed into the VRT trim cleanup.
